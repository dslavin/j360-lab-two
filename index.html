Shanghai Sushi

Shanghai Sushi is a Bloomington company that serves delicious asian fusion creations. Our specialty is cupcakes of all shapes and sizes. We have a storefront downtown on Kirkwood Avenue and also provide catering services.

Our mission is to provide the Bloomington community with fantastic food day in and day out. Our flavor combinations are endless and we add new menu items every day based on customer recommendations.
