Shanghai Sushi

Shanghai Sushi is a Bloomington born company that serves delicious asian fusion creations. Our specialty is sushi and dimsum with our own unique twist. We have a three locations and also provide catering services.

Our mission is to provide the Bloomington community with fantastic food day in and day out. Our flavor combinations are endless and we add new menu items based on customer recommendations.
