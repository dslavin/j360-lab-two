Danielle Slavin, CEO

Danielle is the CEO and founder of Crazy Cupcakes. She started this business in 2015 when she was a student at Indiana University. She graduated with a degree in international business but decided to keep her business local.

Kate Keller, Vice President

Danielle brought Kate onto the Crazy Cupcake team on day 1. They studied business together so she hired Kate to oversee Sales and Marketing. Kate also has a masters degree from the Kelley School of Business.

Sarah Smith, Store Manager

The business got its own storefront 6 months after its start. In January 2016, Sarah began her reign as store manager. She maintains the store and inventory every day. She graduated from Indiana University in 2015 with a degree in business maangement.
